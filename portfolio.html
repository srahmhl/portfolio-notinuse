Step One: Researching

TIP #1

My portfolio needs to appeal to my clients–hiring managers and recruiters. What are their struggles during the hiring process?

Our Objectives

  • Research industry standards and portfolio examples.
  • Design and wireframe the website.
  • Prepare the website for “developer handoff.”
  • Build the website and test.

Step Two: Empathizing

TIP #2

Hiring managers look at dozens if not hundreds of portfolios, resumes, and cover letters during a single hiring round, so their pain points will be the basis of my design decisions.

How might we provide hiring managers with an unforgettable experience to help reduce fatigue, boost our connection across screens, and bring excitement to the initial hiring process?
