Step One: Planning

TIP #1

Travelers often want some sort of safety information for their destination, but finding accurate and trustworthy travel safety information can be challenging.

Is there something not in the market that our app can provide?

Our Objectives

  • User research. Lots of it.
  • Plan and design features for the app.
  • Wireframe the app.
  • Prototype and test.

Step Two: Empathizing

This is Mary! She is a solo traveler, hoping to make the most of her experiences while still staying safe.

How can we best support users like her?

From an anonymous survey we conducted, 47.8% of respondents answered that information on violent crimes was most important for travel safety, however 52.6% searched for COVID statistics before travel.
